About

Who am I?

I'm an Information Technology undergraduate at the University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka, specializing in cybersecurity and computer networking. I'm passionate about understanding how systems work — and how they break.

I completed an internship at Millennium IT ESP as a Network Solutions Intern, and I'm actively involved in ISACA, where I've served in financial and sponsorship coordination roles for HackX Jr. 2024 and CyberZee 2024.

Outside of academics, I work on CTF challenges, freelance IoT and embedded systems projects on Fiverr, and build security tools on GitHub.
